Bio

My name is Moksham Sharma. I was born in Barnala, Punjab, India on August 13th 1999. Barnala is a city in Punjab which is known for its:

  • Textile market
  • Polished woodwork jewelry
  • Traditional jewelry

As a baby, I was always attracted to shiny/reflective things like a bulb, wrist watch, fancy wrist bands etc. My parents would describe me as an infant who loved eating literally anything, could fall asleep in under a minute, and could play for hours. My favorite toy as a toddler was a remote-control car which was given to me by my grandparents for my birthday. I remember being it my favorite toy as I would play non-stop for hours till the battery would die and would go crying to my mother for help to fix it as I as a kid didn’t know that it wasn’t broken.

As I grew older, sports became my hobby. The type of sport never mattered to me, it was always the fun, play time with other kids around me that I used to look forward to every day. According to my mother, it was usually very difficult to convince me to get back home as I always wanted to play more.

Education

I achieved my high school diploma from Ryan international school in 2017. After considering all my options after high school, me and my family decided that I was going to move to Canada to pursue my higher education. I went to the College of New Caledonia in Prince George and graduated with a web and graphic design diploma. This program made me realize that it’s the front/back end of the web that I am more interested in, than the graphic part of it. I am currently a student of the web developer program at SAIT, Calgary.
